My Campus Life

Hackathon

During my campus life, I have experienced many memorable moments. One of the most memorable moments was during a hackathon organized by the USMC. We had to work in a team and provide a prototype within a limited time frame. It was a very challenging and intense experience, but it was also very rewarding when we finally presented the prototype.

Dean's Award Ceremony

Another memorable moment was during a ceremony organized by the university. The event was the Dean's Award Ceremony, where I was recognized for my academic achievements. It was a proud moment for me and my family, and it motivated me to continue striving for excellence in my studies.

Project Shoe Dryer

Another memorable experience was participating in a project to develop a shoe dryer prototype. We worked in a team and had to design and build the prototype. It was a challenging but rewarding experience, and it was great to see our prototype presented to the public.

Adobe Workshop

One of the highlights of my campus life was attending an Adobe workshop. It was an incredible opportunity to learn about the latest creative tools and techniques from industry professionals. The hands-on experience was both educational and inspiring.
